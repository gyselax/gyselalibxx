Detailed Description

template<class CurvilinearToCartesian>
class CartesianSolution< CurvilinearToCartesian >

Define a Cartesian solution of the Poisson equation.

The solution is given by

  • \( \phi (x,y) = C (1+r(x,y))^6 (1 - r(x,y))^6 \cos(2\pi x) \sin(2\pi y) \),

with \(C = 2^{12}1e-4 \).

Its x-derivative is

  • \( \partial_x \phi(x,y) = C \cdot 12 (r(x,y)^2 -1)^5 \cdot r(x,y) \cdot \partial_x r(x,y)\cdot \cos(2\pi x) \sin(2\pi y) - C (r(x,y)^2 -1)^6 \cdot 2\pi \sin(2\pi x) \sin(2\pi y), \)

and its y-derivative,

  • \( \partial_y \phi(x,y) = C \cdot 12 (r(x,y)^2 -1)^5 \cdot r(x,y) \cdot \partial_y r(x,y) \cdot \cos(2\pi x) \sin(2\pi y) + C (r(x,y)^2 -1)^6 \cdot 2\pi \cos(2\pi x) \cos(2\pi y). \)
