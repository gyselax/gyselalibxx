ChargeExchangeRate::ChargeExchangeRate ( double  norm_coeff_rate)
explicit

Creates an instance of the ConstantChargeExchangeRate class.

A polynomial of reaction rate vs. ion temperature is fitted in log-log space. The polynomial fits data from the OPEN-ADAS database for Hydrogen.

Parameters
[in]norm_coeff_rateAll rate coefficients coefficients are normalized so that CX is of order unity at normalized density (n) = 1 and normalized temperature (T) = 1 with n_0 = 1e20 and T_0 = 10 eV. These are typical values for a SOL plasma. The norm_coeff_rate parameter shifts all the reaction rates to modify the source dynamics.
