Detailed Description

template<class Mapping>
class TranslationAdvectionFieldSimulation< Mapping >

Simulation of a translated Gaussian.

Define a simulation with a Gaussian defined by FunctionToBeAdvected_gaussian and a translation advection field defined by ElectrostaticalPotentialSimulation_translation:

  • \( f(x, y) = C \exp\left( - \frac{(x - x_0)^2}{2\sigma_x^2} - \frac{(y - y_0)^2}{2\sigma_y^2} \right)\) with
    • \( C = 1 \),
    • \( x_0 = -0.2 \) ,
    • \( y_0 = -0.2 \) ,
    • \( \sigma_x = 0.1 \) ,
    • \( \sigma_y = 0.1 \) ;

and

  • \( A(x,y) = [v_x, v_y]\),

with \( v_x \) and \( v_y \) constants.
