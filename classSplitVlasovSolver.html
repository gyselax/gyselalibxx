Detailed Description

A class that solves a Vlasov equation using Strang's splitting.

The Vlasov equation is split between two advection equations along the X and Vx directions. The splitting involves solving the x-direction advection first on a time interval of length dt/2, then the vx-direction advection on a tim dt, and then x-direction again on dt/2.
