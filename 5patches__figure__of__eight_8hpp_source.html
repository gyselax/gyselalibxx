3 /*
4  Geometry defined here: 5 * 2D patches.
5  - for all patches:
6  - non-periodic on X and Y;
7  - uniform cubic splines;
8  - uniform Grid1 and Grid2;
9 
10  The geometry describes a figure of eight (the shape of the number eight) such that:
11  - The northern edge of Patch 1 is connected to the western edge of Patch 2
12  - The eastern edge of Patch 4 is connected to the southern edge of Patch 5
13 
14  The outside describes a circle such that:
15  - The X edges (eastern/western) of Patch 1 are connected to the northern edges of Patches 2 and 4
16  - The X edges (eastern/western) of Patch 5 are connected to the southern edges of Patches 2 and 4
17 
18  → → → ↘
19  ↗
20  ↑ ↙ | 1 |↖
21  ↑ -----------------
22  ↖ 2 | 3 | 4
23  ----------------- ↖
24  ↘ | 5 |↗ ↑
25  ↘ ↗
