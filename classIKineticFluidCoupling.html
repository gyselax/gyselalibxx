Operator for applying the source term on the distribution function and neutral density.

The source \(S\) acts on the distribution function following the evolution equation \(df/dt = S_{n,N}(x) * S_v(x,v)\) where \(S_{n,N}(x) = n_N(x) n_e(x) K_i(x) - n_i(x) n_e(x) K_r(x)\) \(S_v(x,v)\) is the sum of order 0 to 2 Hermite polynomials times a Maxwellian velocity distribution function.

The source \(S\) acts on the neutral density following the evolution equation \(dn_N/dt = - S_n,N(x)\)
