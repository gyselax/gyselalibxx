Class ToroidalToCylindrical

template <class Curvilinear2DToCartesian, class Zeta, class Phi>

ClassList > ToroidalToCylindrical

A class describing a coordinate change from a toroidal system of coordinates to a cylindrical system of coordinates. The toroidal coordinates are described by a polar plane and a perpendicular dimension . The cylindrical coordinates are . describe a Cartesian slice. are therefore defined from this slice with a 2D coordinate change operator. is chosen to be equal to to preserve the orientation of the axes (following the right-hand rule).More...
